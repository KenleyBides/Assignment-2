The Great Gatsby

Set in the roaring 1920s, The Great Gatsby tells the story of the mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and his obsession with the beautiful Daisy Buchanan. Through the eyes of narrator Nick Carraway, the novel explores themes of love, wealth, ambition, and the elusive American Dream.
